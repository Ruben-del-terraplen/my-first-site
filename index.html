§ 02 — Approach

I design products by thinking in systems first — connecting research, business strategy, and craft into experiences that scale beyond the screen they're built on. I've led and mentored multidisciplinary teams of 10+ designers across complex product environments to deliver measurable results.

§ 03 — Framework

Framework.

A phased approach shared across projects. Each initiative is unique — methods may vary depending on context.

1

Discovery

  • Analyze data (Adobe, FullStory)
  • Competitive analysis
  • Stakeholder interviews if necessary
  • Design feasibility analysis
  • User tests, surveys
2

Design

  • User journey analysis
  • Touchpoints definition
  • Sketches, wireframes
  • ADA review
  • Stakeholder alignment
  • Internal UX team review
3

Leadership approval

  • Rounds of review planning
  • Presentation preparation
  • Success metrics definition
  • Apply leadership feedback
4

Handoff

  • Deliverables refinement
  • Accessibility, usability, interaction and design definitions
  • Source of truth definitions
5

Design review

  • Collaboration: engaged with engineers during build
  • QA: review against specs
  • Problem-solving: adjust for constraints
  • Iteration: refine from feedback
